# How to Set Up Your Privacy Policy

## Quick Options

### Option 1: Host on Your Website (Recommended)

If you have a website (e.g., `getglory.pk`):

1. **Create a privacy policy page:**
   - Create a new page: `https://www.getglory.pk/privacy-policy`
   - Copy the content from `PRIVACY_POLICY_TEMPLATE.md`
   - Customize it with your actual information
   - Publish the page

2. **Enter the URL in Play Console:**
   - Go back to Play Console
   - Enter: `https://www.getglory.pk/privacy-policy`
   - Click "Save"

### Option 2: Use a Free Privacy Policy Generator

1. **Visit a privacy policy generator:**
   - [Privacy Policy Generator](https://www.privacypolicygenerator.info/)
   - [Termly Privacy Policy Generator](https://termly.io/products/privacy-policy-generator/)
   - [FreePrivacyPolicy.com](https://www.freeprivacypolicy.com/)

2. **Fill in the form:**
   - App name: Get Glory
   - Website URL: Your website (if you have one)
   - Email: Your support email
   - Data collected: Email, name, address, payment info, CV data, ID card data
   - Third-party services: Supabase (backend), Payment processors

3. **Generate and download:**
   - Generate the privacy policy
   - Download or copy the HTML/text

4. **Host it:**
   - Upload to your website, OR
   - Use a free hosting service (see Option 3)

5. **Enter URL in Play Console**

### Option 3: Use Free Hosting (If No Website)

If you don't have a website, you can use:

1. **GitHub Pages (Free):**
   - Create a GitHub repository
   - Create a file: `privacy-policy.html` or `privacy-policy.md`
   - Enable GitHub Pages
   - URL will be: `https://yourusername.github.io/repository-name/privacy-policy`

2. **Google Sites (Free):**
   - Go to [sites.google.com](https://sites.google.com)
   - Create a new site
   - Add your privacy policy content
   - Publish and get the URL

3. **Notion (Free):**
   - Create a Notion page with your privacy policy
   - Make it public
   - Share the public link

### Option 4: Use a Simple Text File (Temporary)

For a quick solution:

1. **Create a simple HTML file:**
   ```html
   <!DOCTYPE html>
   <html>
   <head>
       <title>Privacy Policy - Get Glory</title>
   </head>
   <body>
       <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
       <!-- Paste your privacy policy content here -->
   </body>
   </html>
   ```

2. **Host it anywhere:**
   - Upload to your web hosting
   - Or use one of the free options above

## What to Include in Your Privacy Policy

Based on your app, make sure to mention:

1. **Data You Collect:**
   - User accounts (email, name)
   - CV/resume data
   - ID card information
   - Order/purchase information
   - Payment information

2. **Third-Party Services:**
   - Supabase (for backend and data storage)
   - Payment processors (if applicable)
   - Any analytics services

3. **How Data is Used:**
   - To provide app functionality
   - To process orders
   - To improve the app

4. **Contact Information:**
   - Your support email
   - Your website (if applicable)
